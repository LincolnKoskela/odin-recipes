Chocolate Chip Cookie Skillet Recipe

Deepdish cookie

Why a deep dish warm melted skillet cookie after all?

It's the perfect snack to the chill nights, when the only

thing between you and the tv is melted sweet satisfaction.

Enjoy your favorite Netflix show or your true crime with

the best late night snack, so get snuggy and enjoy.

The cookie is meshed into one big cookie that can be sliced like a pizza

baked using a iron cast skillet, lets go over the ingredients and how to make this!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chocolate waters
  • 1/2 cup plus 2 tablespoons dark brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup (1/2 stick) plus 2 table spoons salter butter, room temp
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons vanilla paste or extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • Flaky sea salt

  1. Place a rack in lowest position in oven; preheat to 375.

    Coarsly chop chocolate, leaving several wafer whole; set aside.

  2. Mix brown sugar, granulated sugar, butter, and kosher salt in a

    large bowl until no large lumps remain. Stir in egg and vanilla until egg

    is incorporated and mixture is very loose and resembles cake batter

    Add flour and baking soda, and mix until dough comes together and no streaks remain.

    Stir in 3/4 chocolate, reserving remaining chocolate, until evenly distributed

  3. Scrape batter into 10' iron cast skillet. Pat batter into skillet with damp hands

    pressing into an even layer so dough extends to sides of pan. Scatter remaining chocolate

    over cookie and sprinkle with sea salt
