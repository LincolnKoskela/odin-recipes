Chocolate Lava Cake

Simple, yet delicious desert recipe that's impressive but easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
  • 4oz bittersweet or semi-sweet chocholate
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 6 tbsp all all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Butter or spray for greasing ramekins

How to Make!

  1. Prep oven and ramekins

    Preheat oven to 425 degree (218C). Butter or spray 4 ramekins and

    lightly dust with cocoa powder

  2. Melt chocolate and butter

    In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter and chocolate in 30 second

    intervals, stirring each time until smooth. Let it cool slightly.

  3. Mix in sugar and eggs

    Whisk in powdered sugar until combined. Then whisk in the eggs and egg yolks

    until smooth and thick.

  4. Add flour, vanilla, salt

    Stir in flour, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt until just combined.

  5. Pour into ramekins

    Divide batter evenly among the prepared ramekins.

  6. Bake

    Bake for 11-13 minutes until the edges are set but the center is still soft.

    Don't overbake! it's supposed to be gooey inside.

  7. Invert and serve

    Let cool for 1 minute. Run a knife around the edges, invert onto plates, and serve immediately.
